German Club is open to all students who are interested in language, culture, and having fun. There is no requirement to be in German. We meet every Friday in W209 at Bartlett High School; which Freshmen can take the Activity Bus for. The latest meetings are held to is 3:30 PM. We manage our trips to Germany through the German Club, as well as any events we host or on-campus events. Our goal for the 2018-2019 school year is to involve the German Club more in the campus community.
